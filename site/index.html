Before you download

Simple setup.

If SSH already works from this Mac to your Hermes host, setup is usually straightforward.

ssh your-host
  • macOS 14 or newer
  • python3 available on the Hermes host
  • Hermes data under the remote user’s ~/.hermes
Trust model

Easy to understand.

No helper service on the host. No gateway API. No separate local copy pretending to be the source of truth.

Current release builds are universal for Apple Silicon and Intel, but they are not notarized yet.
